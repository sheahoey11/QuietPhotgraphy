About

Introducing Myself

Hello everyone, I am Shea Hoey a 24 year old photographer from Cullyhanna in County Armagh.
I have a Panasonic FZ82 Camera which I love using. I am a very friendly and hard-working
individual who enjoys the challenge of capturing the perfect picture. I have taken photo
of weddings, engagements, family parties etc. Anything thing that I can do to make your
experience better I will do. Please enjoy the selections of photos i have taken.

My Love For Photography

My love for taking photos began when I was 10 years old and I received my first camera.
It was'nt the greatest camera in the world, but to me it was enough. I started off by
taking photos of nature and my family. After a few years I got a new camera which was
one of the greatest things to happen to me, my love for photography grew even more than
I thought it could. So here we are now in the present and I am still taking photos and
have created my own Website for my own photographs.

Locations

Warrenpoint/Rostrevor - County Down

This is one of my favourite places to go and take pictures. There is so many things to see and do round here which is such a plus. The mountains overlooking Rostrevor are unbelievable, while the busy town of Warrenpoint can capture some beuatiful photos.

Greenore - County Louth

When visiting Greenore, the views will take your breath away. There is so many fantastic places to capture a picture. Whether it is when you are out exploring the historic village of Greenore, or whether it is on the beach trying to capture that perfect image. If you are lucky enough you might even get the chance to see Finn the Dolphin that is always around the sea at Greenore.

Slieve Gullion - County Armagh

There is nearly to many places at Slieve Gullion where you can capture that stunning photo. There is so much to do here as well whetehr it is on your own or with your family. You can take the scenic drive, stop off whenever you want and get that amazing photo. The views from Slieve Gullion are amazing as they are looking over the whole of Newry and Mourne.

Blackrock - County Louth

The village of Blackrock may be small in size but for activties to do and see it really is quite amazing. The beach offers us some fantasic photo opputunities. Whether the tide is in our out you are in for a special photgraph here. The village itself is a very peaceful and beautiful area with bars, coffee and ice cream shops in plenty.
